This certificate is issued by the Literature, Publishing, and Translation Commission in Saudi Arabia, ensuring that the translators have the skills and standards required to practice the translation profession with a high level of professionalism

The Tarjim initiative aims to support the translation movement in the kingdom of Saudi Arabia, to enrich Arab content with high-value translated works from different languages. And promoting the translation of works into and out of Arabic in order to highlight the connections in knowledge and culture that exist between Saudi Arabia and the rest of the world

Tarjim initiative lines

02

Translated Academic journals, magazines, and cultural articles grants:  This line supports the translation of Academic journals, magazines, and cultural articles specializing in cultural fields. This is accomplished by the Commission by providing funds that cover the price of translation and intellectual property rights

01

Translated books grants:
This line supports the translation of high-quality books by providing grants that cover the costs of intellectual property rights and book translation expenses to Saudi publishing houses

In October 2023, the Literature, Publishing, and Translation Commission collaborated with the King Faisal Center for Research and Islamic Studies to create the UNESCO Chair for Cultural Translation. The chair helps to meet the increased demand for specialized research in the humanities and social sciences, inclusive education, and cultural diversity.  

Its topics will open the way for new research in cultural translation by fostering local, regional, and worldwide collaboration among scholars in translation studies, cultural studies, intangible heritage, the humanities, current technology, and artificial intelligence. This objective will be met by bridging cultural gaps, exploring innovative pathways in cultural translation and creating cooperation between representatives from the academic and artistic industries.  

 The UNESCO Chair for Cultural Translation also aims to launch initiatives to promote scientific research, offer scholarships, organize seminars in cultural translation, enrich current academic theses aimed at developing the concept of "culture," enhance Arabic culture and cultural diversity, and enrich knowledge.

Objectives of the Chair

02

Supporting the next generation of cultural translation professionals by creating and executing projects that improve education, expertise, and research in cultural translation and the humanities. This involves providing fellowships, grants, and public cultural events

01

Encouraging research and partnerships that support cultural translation to bridge cultures

04

Developing innovative policies and practices in cultural translation to widen the definition of culture, increase cultural diversity, and deepen awareness of Arabic culture in all of its components through translation

03

Publishing research, sharing best practices and educational resources related to cultural translation through publications, seminars, workshops, and online platforms to reach a broader audience, and enhancing global understanding of cultural diversity through translation studies

An effort initiated by the Arab League Educational, Cultural, and Scientific Organization, with backing from the Literature, Publishing, and Translation Commission, seeks to establish an observatory to document the Arab translation movement. This program aims to promote the growth of Arabic translations by organizing and coordinating all activities in the sector.       

The center's mission is to gather and provide reliable data and up-to-date statistics on the state and dynamics of the translation industry. This will aid in the establishment of a comprehensive digital database as well as a unified Arab plan for the translation movement throughout the Arab world.

It helps transfer and localize information by providing an innovative data method that incorporates advanced technology. This offers Arab countries with a digital reference that assures the greatest levels of transparency and credibility in data presentation, allowing them to authentically reflect the Arab world's cultural image

The Arabic observatory of translation Objectives

02

Documenting the translation movement in the Arab world to support and advance Arabic translations

01

Coordinating and unifying all translation efforts to contribute to accurately reflect the cultural image of the Arab world

04

Offering a digital reference with the highest levels of transparency and credibility for Arab countries

03

Providing a digital reference with the highest levels of transparency and credibility for Arab countries
